My Journey

I am a professional architect who has been involved in the industry for over 5+ years. My specialties are in the area of interior and exterior design, architecture, and arts. I am good at creative thinking and able to take responsibility and give strong logical reasoning. I will utilize my designing skills to create interactive and meaningful wireframes and designs for digital platforms. While designing I keep in mind that the design is sufficient enough to make them meaningful, unique and interactive.

  • Oct 2022 - Dec 2022

    Google UX Design Professional Certificate

    My main focus on this course was to learn about the interaction that users have with products, like websites and mobile apps in order to make these everyday interactions useful, enjoyable, and accessible.

  • 2019 - Present

    Freelancer

    I am a freelancer working on UX/brand design with a passion for creating meaningful and impactful user experiences. My strength lies in combining creative concepts with technical knowledge to develop user interfaces and products that are both visually pleasing and highly functional. I have a deep understanding of user-centered design principles, user research, usability testing, interface design, prototyping, and design systems.

Vidhyut Urja

Logo and brand design in Adobe Photoshop.

Vidhyut Urja is an electrical shop, consultancy, and contracting service located in Goa, India. They specialize in the sale, and installation of a wide range of electrical products and taking electrical work projects for residence and corporation level, from small household appliances to larger industrial and commercial electrical systems. They are known for their commitment to quality and their customer service.

I was commissioned to create a Name, branding, and logo design for Vidhyut Urja that captures the essence of their business and resonates with their customers. My Logo design was inspired by the electrical energy that is a vital part of the business. I incorporated a vibrant Orange to symbolize the power of electricity and the excitement of innovation. The logo is composed of lightning, wire, and human, which represent the electrical products and services that the company provides.
